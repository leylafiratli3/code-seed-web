TrustClient

We brought our client's idea to life through TrustClient, a platform that allows contractors to rate and review clients, creating accountability in an industry where non-payment, discrimination, and unfair treatment are all too common. When a client doesn't pay, engages in racist behavior, or mistreats workers, contractors can report it here—helping others avoid similar situations and building a safer, more equitable marketplace for everyone.

SoulSeed Festival

SoulSeed Festival is a beautiful celebration of cultural exchange that brings together the Yawanawa indigenous tribe from the Amazon and Sufi culture through the practice of sema (whirling dervish ceremonies) and music in Muğla, Türkiye. We created a website that captures the spirit of this unique gathering—where ancient traditions meet, stories are shared, and communities connect across continents. It's a digital space that honors both cultures and invites visitors to experience this transformative event.

ListForge

Our lead developer Bryce has been playing Warhammer since childhood. When he returned to the hobby after years away, he discovered that players still lacked the right tools to create and manage their army lists efficiently. So, we created ListForge—a cross-platform app that syncs seamlessly across iOS, macOS, Android, Windows, and Linux. ListForge has now grown into a trusted tool for over 20,000 tabletop gaming enthusiasts worldwide.
