About Us Hakkımızda

We're engineers who've built systems for major tech platforms, and left due to ethical concerns. We bring the same professional standards, rigorous processes, and technical excellence — but without supporting surveillance capitalism, environmental impact, and exploitative practices. Our mission is to build technology that serves people without extracting from them. Büyük teknoloji platformları için sistemler kuran mühendisleriz ve etik kaygılar nedeniyle ayrıldık.
