A Little Celebration: One Year of ListForge

ListForge is an intuitive app that helps tabletop players organize and share their game lists. It is slowly becoming the industry standard for tabletop players and we are so proud of its progress. Watching it grow over the last year has been a real joy for us.

On the Horizon: Rites of Accord

We are building a multiplayer game called Rites of Accord. Our intention is to create a hopeful but realistic imagination of what the world might look like in a century from now and plant the seed for a new world, with better systems that benefit everyone, together.

It is a labor of love, and we cannot wait to share more.

We are aiming to have it available to play by the end of 2026. More soon.
