Example Setup

The Client

A boutique law firm with 5 attorneys specializing in commercial real estate and corporate transactions.

The Problem

Each attorney was spending 10-15 hours per week on initial document review for due diligence. Contract comparison across deal versions was manual and error-prone. Junior associates were burning out on repetitive review work instead of developing higher-level skills.

The Setup

  • —AI contract review pipeline configured to pre-screen documents and flag non-standard clauses
  • —Automated comparison reports between contract versions
  • —Legal research assistant set up to summarize relevant case law
  • —Draft client memo generation added to the workflow

The Result

  • —Initial document review time reduced by 60%
  • —Junior associates shifted to substantive analysis instead of clause-by-clause reading
  • —Client memo turnaround: 2 days → same-day
  • —30% more matters handled without adding headcount
