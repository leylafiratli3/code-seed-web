Your Website Should Match the Quality of Your Work.

Most businesses face the same frustrating choice: pay for a cheap template that looks like everyone else's site, or commit to a full agency build that blows the budget. The template route is fast, but your clients can tell it's generic. The custom route looks great, but the cost and timeline are hard to justify when you're trying to grow.

There should be something in between. That's exactly what we built.

CodeSeed designs and develops professional websites with fully custom design, modern architecture, and a simple content dashboard — so you can update your own text, images, and pages without touching code or calling a developer.

01

What You Bring to the Table

Here's how we keep the price fair and the process smooth.

You provide your content

Text, images, and any branding assets (logo, colors, fonts). We design and build; you bring the words and visuals. If you need help with copywriting or photography, we can recommend partners.

You pay for your own hosting

Typically ~$10/month through providers like Railway, Vercel, or Netlify. We'll set it up for you during the build — you just pay the hosting provider directly.

Post-launch changes are hourly

After handoff, anything beyond what's editable in your dashboard is billed at our hourly support rate. No retainer, no commitment — just reach out when you need help.
