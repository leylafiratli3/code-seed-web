One of Our Tools: OpenClaw

Open-source AI agent, self-hosted

OpenClaw is an open-source, self-hosted AI agent that runs on your own hardware. It accepts natural-language commands and executes them through modular plugins called "skills" — from automated crypto trading and portfolio monitoring to market analysis and alerts. It's particularly powerful for traders who want 24/7 AI-powered market operations without cloud dependencies.

What we do with it:

  • Full installation and configuration
  • Skill selection and setup (trading, monitoring, alerts)
  • Exchange API integration (Binance, Coinbase, etc.)
  • Risk management configuration
  • Ongoing optimization

OpenClaw is one of many tools in our arsenal. We're tool-agnostic — we find what works best for your specific situation.
