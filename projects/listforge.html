Project Overview

The Challenge

Tabletop gaming enthusiasts needed a more intuitive and efficient way to build and manage their army lists. Traditional methods were cumbersome, time-consuming, and often led to errors in point calculations and rule compliance.

The challenge was to create a cross-platform solution that could handle complex game mechanics while remaining user-friendly for both casual and competitive players.

Our Solution

ListForge is a sophisticated army building platform that combines powerful data management with an intuitive interface. The app streamlines the army creation process while ensuring accuracy and compliance with game rules.

Built with a mobile-first approach, ListForge provides a seamless experience across all major platforms, allowing players to build and manage their lists anywhere, anytime.

Key Features

Advanced Data Management

Real-time validation and comprehensive data management system that ensures accuracy and prevents rule violations.

Cross-Platform Support

Available on iOS, macOS, Android, Windows, and Linux with synchronized data across all devices.

Clean Design & Intuitive Interface

User-friendly interface with clean design principles that make army building simple and enjoyable for users.

Community Features

Collaborative features for sharing lists and engaging with the gaming community.

Real-Time Sync

Automatic synchronization across all devices ensures your lists are always up to date.

Rule Compliance

Built-in validation ensures all army lists comply with game rules and point limits.
