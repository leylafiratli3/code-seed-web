Project Overview

The Challenge

Tabletop gaming enthusiasts needed a more intuitive and efficient way to build and manage their army lists. Traditional methods were cumbersome, time-consuming, and often led to errors in point calculations and rule compliance.

The challenge was to create a cross-platform solution that could handle complex game mechanics while remaining user-friendly for both casual and competitive players.

Our Solution

ListForge is a sophisticated army building platform that combines powerful data management with an intuitive interface. The app streamlines an accurate army creation process, makes sharing lists easy, provides users in-game support, and allows for mathmatical build simulations.

Built with a mobile-first approach, ListForge provides a seamless experience across all major platforms, allowing players to build and manage their lists anywhere, anytime.
