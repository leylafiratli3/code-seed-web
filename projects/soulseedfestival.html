SoulSeed Festival Website

A Modern, Immersive Web Experience for a Transformative Festival

Visit Website

Project Overview

The SoulSeed Festival team needed a visually striking, easy-to-update website to showcase their unique music and healing arts festival. The site had to communicate the festival's spirit, provide clear program and ticketing info, and work beautifully on all devices.

Our goal: deliver a fast, immersive, and accessible web experience that inspires visitors to join the journey.

Design & UX Approach

  • Design-to-Code Collaboration: The visual and UX design for soulseedfestival.com was provided by the client's creative team. CodeSeed's role was to faithfully translate these Figma/Sketch layouts into pixel-perfect, responsive code.
  • Close communication: Worked closely with designers to clarify intent, resolve edge cases, and ensure the final site matched the original vision across all devices.
  • Mobile-first layout: Implemented a responsive grid and flexible sections for seamless experience on any screen size.
  • Immersive visuals: Used gradients, iconography, and section alternation as specified in the design system.
  • Accessible, maintainable code: Ensured all design elements were implemented with semantic HTML and scalable CSS for future updates.

Technical Implementation

  • HTML5 & Tailwind CSS: Semantic markup and utility-first styling for rapid, maintainable development.
  • Custom JavaScript: For mobile menu, smooth scrolling, and interactive elements.
  • Performance optimized: Minimal dependencies, lazy loading, and compressed assets.
  • SEO & accessibility: Meta tags, alt text, and keyboard navigation support.
  • Easy content updates: Modular section structure for quick edits and future expansion.

Key Features

Design-to-Code Implementation

Converted provided Figma/Sketch designs into pixel-perfect, responsive HTML/CSS/JS.

Custom Visual Identity

Soulful color scheme, iconography, and modern typography as specified by the design team.

Fast & Lightweight

Minimal dependencies, fast load times, and smooth navigation.

SEO & Accessibility

Meta tags, alt text, and keyboard navigation for broad reach.

Easy to Update

Modular HTML sections for quick content changes and future growth.

Clear Contact & CTA

Prominent ticketing and contact options for maximum engagement.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Multilingual content: Designed for easy future addition of Turkish/English toggle.
  • Festival spirit online: Used gradients, icons, and alternating backgrounds to evoke the event's energy.
  • Mobile navigation: Custom JS for a smooth, accessible mobile menu.
  • Content flexibility: Modular HTML lets organizers update program, tickets, and info with minimal dev work.

Outcome & Impact

The new SoulSeed Festival website launched on time and received praise for its clarity, beauty, and ease of use. Organizers can now update content quickly, and visitors enjoy a fast, immersive experience on any device.
