Project Overview

Engineered a high-performance video hosting platform for Semayoga.com, revolutionizing member engagement through unlimited content access and intelligent content management.

The platform enables seamless delivery of yoga content to members while providing robust administrative tools for content management and user engagement tracking.

Technical Challenge

Key challenges included:

  • Implementing unlimited video hosting with robust custom filtering options
  • Creating an automated video management system for content delivery
  • Developing a scalable infrastructure for daily video uploads
  • Ensuring high-quality video streaming across different devices
  • Building an intuitive interface for both members and administrators

Core Features

  • • Advanced content filtering and search functionality
  • • Automated video processing and optimization pipeline
